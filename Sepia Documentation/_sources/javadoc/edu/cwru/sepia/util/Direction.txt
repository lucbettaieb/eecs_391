Direction
=========

.. java:package:: edu.cwru.sepia.util
   :noindex:

.. java:type:: public enum Direction

   A basic enumeration of the eight directions in which a primitive action can be taken. Directions are treated the same as screen coordinates: East is positive x, South is positive y.

   :author: Tim
